How will I get a Personal Code?

You will receive your Personal Code in the mail at your primary address on file with your school. Just look for the bright green envelope and please make sure your address is up to date. The personal code is also emailed to you using the email address on file with your school.

How is my money delivered?

First, your school draws funds from the respective loan and/or grant provider and applies it to your student account. Once this information is verified, it is sent to BankMobile. Once the specific refund information is received, it is processed and disbursed according to your selection.
